Conclusion

Among all the four methods, DFS gives the worst path, but its performance is highly dependent to the visit order. For a well selected order, DFS can be faster than BFS and doesn't need to visit the entire space. BFS tends to visit the most of the section, so it is the most time consuming. A-Star has a search direction to the goal, and it always takes less time than BFS. Greedy-Best-Search is the most efficient method, but it will not always give the most optimal path. It will not give the shortest path if the path is opposite to the goal direction, and in this condition it will require more time to find the path.
